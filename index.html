Cuisine

We are a multi cuisine restuarant serving Continental, Italian, Mexican, Beverages, Desserts, Shake. You also have the liberty to order from the various restaurants present in the Hotel including the lip-smacking Indian Cuisine from Mirch Masala, Chinese from Shanghai Express and Alcoholic Drinks from our very own Ivy Bar.
